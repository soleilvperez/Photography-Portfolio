Soleil Perez Photographer

I’m a Mexican- American photographer raised in Brooklyn who has a keen eye for visual art. I aim to capture the beauty, emotion, and uniqueness of a moment through color and composition. If I make it sound whimsical and poetic, that's because I love to create photos that reflect just that! Art and Magic! I love to create! Although my specialty is portraiture, I love art with meaning and find beauty in everything, so I enjoy capturing all types of events and photography genres. When I’m not taking photos you can find me cuddling with my dog, sketching, starting a painting, eating a torta, or sipping bubble tea!
